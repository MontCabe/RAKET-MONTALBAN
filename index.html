    About Montalban

    Raket Montalban is a community-based platform designed to connect residents of Montalban, Rizal with trusted local service providers and job opportunities. Our goal is to make it easier for individuals to find skilled workers for everyday needs


    Raket Montalban serves as a centralized digital space where users can easily find, offer, and manage various types of rakets. The platform promotes local employment growth by empowering workers to showcase their skills while helping clients access reliable services quickly and conveniently. Using Raket Montalban is simple— users can create an account for free and gain access to the following features:


    For JobSeekers/Applicants:

    • Publish and manage your resume online
    • Showcase your skills and qualifications to local employers
    • Receive job invitations from employers based on your profile
    • Provide ratings and feedback to employers after the job is completed

    For Employers/Service Providers:

    • Search for applicants through published resumes
    • Match employees based on required skills and qualifications
    • Hire applicants once verified and accepted
    • Give ratings and feedback to employees after the job is completed

    Documentary Requirements for Accreditation:

    • NBI Clearance
    • Police Clearance
    • Proof of Insurance
    • TIN
